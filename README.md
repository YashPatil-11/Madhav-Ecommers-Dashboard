📊 Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze e-commerce sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales, profit, customer behavior, product categories, payment methods, and regional performance using KPIs, interactive filters, and visualizations for effective business decision-making
📝 Project Overview

The Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze e-commerce sales data and generate meaningful business insights. It enables users to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product demand, and regional sales using interactive visualizations and KPIs.

🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze overall sales performance.

Monitor monthly profit trends.

Identify top-selling product categories.

Compare sales across different states.

Analyze customer purchasing behavior.

Understand preferred payment methods.

Support data-driven business decisions.

Clothing category contributed the highest quantity sold.

COD was the most preferred payment mode.

November showed the highest monthly profit.

A small group of customers generated the highest revenue.
